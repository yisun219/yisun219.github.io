About

I am an undergraduate student at the School of Microelectronics, South China University of Technology. During freshman and sophomore years, I had research experience with Prof. Xiang Yi, Prof. Li Gao, Prof. Guangyin Feng and Prof. Enyi Yao. Currently, I am doing meaningful projects with Prof. Jieru Zhao at School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), Dr. Fengshi Tian (Supervised by Prof. Tim CHENG and Prof. Chi-Ying TSUI) at Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering (ECE), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) and Dr. Yizhuo Wu (Supervised by Prof. Chang Gao) at Department of Microelectronics, TU Delft.

I will spend the Spring 2026 semester as an exchange student at the University of California, Berkeley.

My current research focuses on high performance AI chip, computer architecture and computer systems, EDA and AI for RFIC.
